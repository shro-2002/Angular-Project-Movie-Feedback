Rotten Tomatoes
Rotten Tomatoes is a website that aggregates reviews of movies and TV shows from critics. The site was launched in 1998 by three undergraduate students at the University of California, Berkeley. Rotten Tomatoes collects reviews from critics who publish at outlets worldwide. The reviews are rated either Fresh or Rotten, which contributes to a film or TV show's Tomatometer score. The score is what percentage of those critics rated the movie 6/10 or higher.
